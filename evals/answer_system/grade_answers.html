Reference (Opus)

According to the passage, the HPA axis (hypothalamic pituitary adrenal axis) is associated with fear and anxiety, and with stress. The speaker states that anyone who has a problem with stress has a problem with that circuit, the HPA axis.

RAG answer

Based on the documents, the HPA axis — the hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal axis — is the axis for fear and anxiety. It is the key system when discussing stress, and anyone who has a problem with stress has a problem with this circuit, the HPA axis.

In terms of its physiological mechanism, there is an interaction between norepinephrine in the brain and in the adrenal gland. It all starts with norepinephrine sent out from the locus coeruleus to activate the release of corticotropin hormone, and then norepinephrine is also sent out from the adrenal medulla to the heart, setting the heart rate. Cortisol coming from the adrenal cortex also goes to the heart — specifically the SA node (sinoatrial node) — speeding up the heart rate.
